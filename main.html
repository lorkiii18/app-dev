About

Building Christ-centered futures since 2021

Our History

Founded in 2021 by the late Mr. Wilfredo G. Ventura, Holy Family Academy began its journey in Sta. Maria, Bulacan. Under the leadership of President Ms. Rizza I. Ventura, we've grown to two campuses:

Main Campus Sitio Hulo, Balasing, Sta. Maria, Bulacan
Catmon Campus Sitio Hulo, Catmon, Sta. Maria, Bulacan (offering JHS to SHS programs)

Our students - proudly called FAMILIANS - enjoy diverse academic tracks including STEM, ABM, GAS, HUMSS, and TVL-ICT.
